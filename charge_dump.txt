Document Purpose
This document constitutes the official Business Requirements Document (BRD) and Functional Specification Document (FSD) for the IntraConnect Platform. It serves as the single source of truth for all stakeholders — Product, Engineering, QA, and Operations — and is the authoritative reference for development, testing, and acceptance.

Audience

Software Engineers – for implementation guidance and technical requirements

Project Managers – for scope management and delivery planning

Executive Summary
IntraConnect is a modular, enterprise-grade intranet and employee management platform designed to centralize employee operations, digitize internal workflows, and provide real-time organizational visibility. The platform replaces fragmented tools and manual processes with a unified digital workspace tailored to small and medium-sized organizations.

Strategic Objectives
Digitize all HR and operational workflows — eliminating paper-based processes
Provide a unified employee experience across departments and roles
Enforce role-based access control (RBAC) across all platform modules
Enable real-time decision-making through configurable dashboards
Ensure full auditability and compliance at every operational touchpoint
Build a scalable, modular architecture ready for future expansion

Roles & Access Control Model
The IntraConnect platform operates on a strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) model. All permissions are enforced at the backend layer — frontend restrictions alone are insufficient and must not be relied upon for security.

System Roles

RBAC Enforcement Rules
Every API endpoint must validate the requesting user's role and permissions before processing.
Role assignments are managed exclusively by HR Admin or System Admin.
Permission changes take effect immediately upon role update.
Each employee must be assigned exactly one role at account creation.
All access attempts — successful or failed — are logged for audit purposes.

Single-use only — invalidated immediately after first use
